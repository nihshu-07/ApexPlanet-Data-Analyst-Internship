## Key Business Insights

* Technology category generated the highest revenue.
* Higher discount levels frequently caused negative profit.
* Orders with discounts above 40% often resulted in losses.
* Same Day shipping had the highest logistics cost.
* Profitability varied significantly across regions.

---

## Statistical Validation

A hypothesis test was performed to validate whether high discount orders generate lower profit than low discount orders.

### Hypothesis

* Null Hypothesis (H0): There is no significant difference in profit between low-discount and high discount orders.
* Alternative Hypothesis (H1): High discount orders generate significantly lower profit.

### Result

The t-test produced a p-value less than 0.05, so the null hypothesis was rejected.

Conclusion:

> Orders with higher discounts generate significantly lower profit.
